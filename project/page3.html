Why is it important?

Becoming educated about climate change, pollution, and environmental issues in general is the first step. Lets look at pollution in particular. Not only is pollution killing our planet and animals but it is also killing our race. Pollution is one of the biggest killers, affecting more than 100 million people worldwide. Even though pollution is a worldwide issue the United States is responsible for creating 30% of the worlds waste and uses 25%of the worlds natural resources.

Products that can help conquer climate change:

Hydro Flask Water bottle

Any reusable water bottle will work. Hydroflask are great reusable waterbottles that hold more than the standard plastic waterbottle and keeps your liquids cold for hours. These water bottles come in all different shapes and sizes with a wide range of colors and different acessories.

Bamboo toothbrush

A toothbrush is something you use at least twice a day everyday that actually causes a lot of pollution. Because toothbrushes are made out of plastic and thrown away ever so often that they can become damaging to the earth. Bamboo toothbrushes eliminate the pollution factor becuase the are compostable.

Reusable Grocery or tote bags

Although some states like New York have eliminated plastic bags not everyone has hopped on this train yet. plastic bags can be found in oceans, forests, and even be the cause of killing animals. A simple switch to resuable bags can make a huge impact.

Metal Straws

Straws not only create a lot of pollution but they also create a threat to animals especially ocean animals. Metal or glass straws are an easy fix to saving our planet alongwith saving some inncocent animals life.

Reusable Coffee Cups

Now this goes hand in hand with the reusable waterbottles. And although a lot of coffee shops have switched to environmentally friendly products a lot have not. It is always a good idea to bring your own cup and sometimes the coffee shop will even give you a discount.

Eco Friendly Sponges

It's little things like sponges that you use almost everyday that can easily be switched to something that will help the planet rather than hurt it.

Environmentally Friendly Laundry Detergent

Laundry detergent is something that most people dont think about, but where do you think the big plastic bottles that the detergent comes from goes. Those big detergent bottles are often found on beaches and in the ocean. Switching to a more environmentally friendly detergent is the way to go.
